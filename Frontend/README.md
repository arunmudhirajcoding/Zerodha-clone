### terminology
- **equity**: the value of the shares issued by a company
- **Holdings**: the total number of shares owned by an investor

- **positions** : which give threesold to sell or buy the shares

- **funds**: the amount of money available for trading

- **orders**: the instructions to buy or sell shares at a specific price and quantity

- **margin**: the amount of money an investor needs to deposit to open a position
